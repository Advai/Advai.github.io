The Gender Ratio and Race Distribution for every major at UIUC since 1980!

By: Advai Podduturi and Aj Federici


The gender gap, that heavily plagued the early 2000s, is beginning to close. This visualization contains gender and race distributions for every major at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I hope this will help you better understand the demographics at UIUC better and reflect the changes the university has seen in the past 38 years.

Hover each bar in the bar graph to see exact values as well as percentage of that race in the major. Hover over a line in the line chart to see the major name.

Note: The M / F Ratios do not account for non-binary gendered students, so the total will not always be the sum of the male and female students. As the data is not 100 percent clean, there might be a few errors (due to the dataset being horribly inconsistent and unreliable) that even I did not catch.
